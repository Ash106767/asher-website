A Wilson Cloud Chamber that makes the paths of radioactive decay visible.

Read more

This project was inspired by the design of various modern millitary equipment, and the mysterious UI design of Star Wars equipment. The most challenging parts of this project was suprisingly not the chamber itself, but dealing with the warping that comes with prints so large.

  • Materials: PLA, peltier plate, copper, thermometer, heat sink, voltage amplifier, power supply.
  • Tools: BambuLabs A1, soldering iron, screwdriver kit, drill.
  • Time: Probably 20 hours spread over a month or so.
